# 🌍 Global E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

A professional **Streamlit-based analytics dashboard** for exploring global e-commerce performance, identifying profitable and loss-making patterns, and generating business-ready insights from transactional data.

## Executive Summary

This project delivers a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow using **Python, SQL, Plotly, and Streamlit**.  
It transforms raw e-commerce transaction data into a polished business intelligence experience with interactive dashboards, 3D visual analytics, AI-style insights, downloadable reports, and responsive UI for both desktop and mobile.

The goal is to help stakeholders:

- understand sales performance across regions and categories
- identify profitable and loss-making products
- analyze customer and segment behavior
- detect discount and margin risks
- forecast future sales trends
- support data-driven business decisions with clear visual storytelling

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- USA generated the highest sales contribution
- Technology was one of the strongest revenue categories
- Some high-selling products showed weaker profit margins
- Several regions contributed less to global revenue
- Customer revenue was concentrated among a small number of top buyers
- Discounting influenced profit behavior in several categories
- Sales forecasting indicated continued growth potential
- Profitability varied significantly across categories and products
